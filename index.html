Opinions

  • “Make an effort to find this place!”

    Stumbled across Greengotts by accident. Make an effort to find it if you like big, healthy, tasty salad with lots of variety. Shrimp Salad with chilli ranch sauce, at $6.75, is a must.

  • “Great fresh, healthy, fast and clean!”

    This was just a block from our hotel! It was a great start to our weekend trip. We are always searching for vegan restaurants. I had a non vegan fall special, curry Chick pea bowl which had chicken. It was delicious but very homogeneously yellow. My daughter's bowl was beautiful, mushroom and greens bowl. The restaurant has a large, bright room to sit and enjoy the meal.

  • “So much yum!”

    We were a little put off this place to start with as there was a massive line but when we came back a little after the lunch rush, we were impressed. Very fresh, very easy to order, very yum! Ate here a number of teams as it was great value for good fresh food.
